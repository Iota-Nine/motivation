# Drop your assets here

## Music
Put your track as: `music.mp3` (this exact name)

## Hologram / anime images
Put images in `holograms/`:
- `holograms/01.png` (or .jpg / .webp)
- optional: `02.png`, `03.png` later for rotation

Or place a single image as `vertix.png` / `vertix.jpg` in this folder.
